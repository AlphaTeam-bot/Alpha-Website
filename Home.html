Today's innovations shape a better future

The Alpha Team is a student-run group of motivated undergraduate and graduate students from the Pyramids Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology. From Mechatronics engineering department and Electronics and communication engineering department, the diverse group shares a common passion for creating inovative projects.

The team aims to learn critical skills, encourage collaborative teamwork, focus on robotics competitions, and explore new technology.
